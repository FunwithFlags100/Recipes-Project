Recipe for Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types,[2] made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings such as rag√π (ground meats and tomato sauce), vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and seasonings and spices, like Italian seasoning, such as garlic, oregano and basil.[3] The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted after baking. Typically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in an oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square portions.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Riccota Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps for Cooking

  1. Cook ground beef until fully cooked and season with beef seasoning of your choice.
  2. In a pot, start boiling water and season water with salt until salty. Finally, place the pasta into the pot until the pasta is nice and floppy
  3. Once you have cooked the pasta and ground beef, start layering the dish.
  4. Start with a bed of pasta, place ground beef on top, tomato sauce, and assortment of cheeses.
  5. Cover with another layer of pasta.
  6. Keeping repeating the layering process until your desired height is achieved.
  7. Place in oven until cheese is melted.
